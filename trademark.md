# Trademark Policy

This document outlines the policy regarding the use of the trademarks, logos, and service names associated with **DeployBerry™** (hereafter referred to as "the Project"). 

While the source code of the Project is made freely available under the GNU Affero General Public License v3 (AGPLv3) (via the [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md) file), copyright licenses do **not** grant permission to use our trademarks. This policy ensures our brand identity remains clear, trusted, and uncompromised.

## 1. Permitted Uses (No Permission Required)

You may use the name **DeployBerry** without our explicit permission in the following scenarios:

*   **Verbatim Redistribution:** You are redistributing unmodified, official binaries or container images compiled directly by the official Project maintainers.
*   **Descriptive Use:** You are describing your own software or services as being "compatible with," "designed for," or "an add-on for" the Project (e.g., *"MyModule: A Kubernetes add-on designed for DeployBerry"*).
*   **Educational Use:** You are mentioning the Project in blogs, tutorials, videos, or academic papers for educational purposes.

## 2. Prohibited Uses (Requires Explicit Permission)

You **cannot** use our name, trademarks, or logos in the following scenarios without direct written permission from the Project owners:

*   **Modified Versions (Forks):** If you fork, alter, or re-code any part of the Project's source code, you **must change the name of the application** and remove all official logos from your distribution. You cannot distribute a modified version under the name **DeployBerry**.
*   **Cloud & Marketplace Hosting Branding:** Cloud providers or hosting platforms may list our AGPLv3 application in their marketplace or automated deployment engines. However, they **cannot** brand their commercial service in a way that implies an official partnership or endorsement (e.g., you cannot call a service *"Official DeployBerry Cloud Hosting"* or *"DeployBerry by DigitalOcean"*). They may use descriptive titles like *"Hosting for DeployBerry"*.
*   **Domain Names:** You may not register a domain name that includes our project name (e.g., `deployberrycloud.com` or `buydeployberry.net`) to sell commercial services.

## 3. Visual Branding and Logos

The official logos, graphics, and color schemes used in the frontend user interface (Vue/Nuxt) are protected brand assets. If you are distributing a fork or an alternative version of this software:
1. You must strip out the official logos (including the logo with the trademark symbol ™).
2. You must replace them with your own distinct branding to ensure users are not confused about who is hosting or maintaining the application.
